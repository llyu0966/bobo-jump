BOBO JUMP

Developer: Liyu Lin

About

The Bobo Jump game follows Bobo's adventure. He likes to collect diamonds ◆ and he is not afraid of all kinds of dangers.

He likes to jump up and down and likes to challenge himself.

Every game has three lives, and when all lives are finished, the game is over.

Instructions

  • Press "← / →" to move the player go forward and backward
  • Press the "Space" key to jump
  • Press "ctrl/cmd + R" to start a new game
  • Press "cmd + W" to close the window
